500 Years in the making

From a 5-room hostel that still stands on the same site, we’ve grown into a 56-room de luxe hotel in Mactan Island, where Portuguese explorer Ferdinand Magellan claimed the island kingdoms for Spain on 1521.

On the 500th anniversary of that occasion, 1521 Hotel opens its doors to an unending stream of travelers ready to discover Cebu, the undisputed Queen City of the South. In close proximity to the international airport and seaport, we are in the middle of a bustling resort island that remains the gateway to the entire region half a millennium later.

The roadmap to your adventure is written in the murals in our original site, the stories of our staff, the food that we serve, and the sights we will bring you to.
